What are internet protocols?

These are methods or protocols by wich data is sent from one computer to another on the internet.

Types of internet protocols

TCP/IP(Transmission control Protocol/internet protocol)
Organizations use TCP to transfer content such as files,text,images and emails because it garantees that the packet will be delivered accurately and in the correct order
SMTP(Simple Mail Transfer protocol)
This allows a device to send and deploy email to a remote endpoint with a TCP connection
HTTP(hyper text transfer protocol)
This enables systems to communicate on the world wide web.
